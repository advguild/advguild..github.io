Adventurer! Your journey to glory awaits!

Welcome to the Adventurer’s Guild! We are a large organization that spans most of the civilized world, and employ hundreds, even thousands of aspiring vagabonds, mercenaries, and heroes across Golarion. Because of this, it is regrettably impossible to commit resources to provide individual training for new members and recruits. You’re on your own, so good luck!

-Captain Furlow, Leader of the Adventurers Guild
