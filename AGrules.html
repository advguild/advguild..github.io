Tips For Getting Started

  1. Character Generation: First character starts at Level one-three,
  2. subsequent characters can be made up to three levels lower than your highest leveled character (excluding dead characters).
  3. Ryan is God.
  4. All books are allowed, any weird 3rd Party content should be run by the DMs Banana.
  5. Game announcements will be made on #ooc-chat (discord), if you want to play in a specific game, message that you're in on that DM's game chat, and further details will be available there.
  6. Point buy for character gen. Standard 5e point buy rules. Use this if you're confused
  7. Definitely consider giving your character a backstory and goals, cause that stuff can be very easily integrated into adventures.
